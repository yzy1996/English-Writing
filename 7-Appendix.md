## 在正文中引用 Appendix

| 引用方式                                                     |
| :----------------------------------------------------------- |

| We refer the reader to Appendix X for the full proof / derivation. |

| More implementation details are provided in Appendix A.      |
| Please refer to Sec. A of the supplementary for more experimental results. |
| Here we discussed the two most relevant to our work. In Appendix A we review additional papers. |
